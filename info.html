About Kasgaon

Kasgaon is a small Village/hamlet in Shahapur Taluka in Thane District of Maharashtra State, India. It comes under Kasagaon Panchayath. It belongs to Konkan region . It belongs to Konkan Division . It is located 63 KM towards East from District head quarters Thane. 86 KM from State capital Mumbai


Kasgaon Pin code is 421601 and postal head office is Shahapur (Thane).
Kasgaon is surrounded by Murbad Taluka towards South , Kalyan Taluka towards west , Wada Taluka towards west , Ulhasnagar Taluka towards South .
Kalyan , Ulhasnagar , Ambernath , Bhiwandi are the near by Cities to Kasgaon.
